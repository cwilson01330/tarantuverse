## Full Description

**Tarantuverse** is the ultimate companion app for tarantula keepers, breeders, and enthusiasts. Whether you have one tarantula or a hundred, Tarantuverse helps you track every aspect of your collection with powerful features designed specifically for invertebrate husbandry.

### 🕷️ COLLECTION MANAGEMENT
• Track unlimited tarantulas with detailed profiles
• Record common names, scientific names, sex, and acquisition details
• Upload unlimited photos for each tarantula
• Organize your collection with powerful search and filtering
• Visual dashboard with collection statistics

### 📊 COMPREHENSIVE TRACKING
• **Feeding Logs**: Track what, when, and whether your T accepted food
• **Molt Logs**: Record premolt behavior, molt dates, and growth measurements
• **Substrate Changes**: Log maintenance with dates and reasons
• **Growth Analytics**: Visualize weight and leg span progression over time
• **Smart Insights**: Get feeding acceptance rates, streaks, and predictions

### 🧬 BREEDING MODULE (Premium)
• **Pairing Management**: Track breeding attempts with detailed notes
• **Egg Sac Monitoring**: Log incubation conditions and development stages
• **Offspring Tracking**: Manage individual offspring records and sales
• Complete lineage tracking for serious breeders

### 📖 SPECIES DATABASE
• Access comprehensive care sheets for 100+ species
• Detailed husbandry requirements (temperature, humidity, enclosure specs)
• Feeding schedules by life stage (sling, juvenile, adult)
• Behavioral traits and temperament information
• Safety information (urticating hairs, venom warnings)
• Native region and growth rate data

### 🌐 COMMUNITY FEATURES
• **Forums**: Connect with other keepers in category-based discussions
• **Activity Feed**: See what the community is up to
• **Direct Messaging**: Chat privately with other enthusiasts
• **Keeper Profiles**: Share your collection and expertise
• **Follow System**: Stay updated with your favorite keepers

### 📈 ADVANCED ANALYTICS
• Visual charts showing growth trends over time
• Feeding statistics with color-coded status indicators
• Premolt predictions based on feeding behavior
• Collection insights and summaries
• Export your data (CSV/PDF) - Premium feature

## Press Kit / Media Description

**Tarantuverse** is a comprehensive mobile and web application designed specifically for tarantula keepers and breeders. Launched in 2024, the app addresses the unique needs of the invertebrate keeping community by providing specialized tracking tools for feeding, molting, substrate maintenance, and breeding projects.

With over 100 species care sheets, advanced analytics, and a thriving community platform, Tarantuverse serves everyone from first-time keepers to professional breeders. The app features a generous free tier (15 tarantulas) and flexible premium options for unlimited tracking and breeding features.

Built with modern technology (React Native, Next.js, FastAPI) and designed with user privacy in mind, Tarantuverse sets a new standard for exotic pet management applications.

**Founded**: 2024
**Platform**: iOS, Android, Web
**Pricing**: Free with premium options
**Target Audience**: Tarantula keepers, breeders, enthusiasts
**Unique Features**: Breeding module, species database, premolt predictions
